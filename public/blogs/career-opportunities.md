Vulnerability Scanning

Penetration testing - A simulated cyber attack against your computer system to check exploitable vulnerabilities. 

Penetration testing life cycle

1. recon - discover the target
2. enumeration or scanning - probing the defenses
3. gaining access - breaking in
4. maintaining access - Securing out foothold
5. covering tracks - erasing out footprint
6. reporting - documentation and reporting
