CIA TRIAD- 

CIA triad

- confidentiality
    - info must only be avail to authorized parties like messages, personal data, healthcare records, Intellectual property.
    - Access control: Passwords, Two-factor authentication, Data Encryption.
- integrity
    - information must remain consistent, trustworthy and accurate examples are bank transact, document editing, online reviews, database management
    - access control: data validation, version control system
- availability
    - info must remain accessible to authorized parties even during failures examples: healthcare systems, online shopping website, cloud services
    - access control: Denial of service(DDoS)Protection
    - backups
    - load balancer
